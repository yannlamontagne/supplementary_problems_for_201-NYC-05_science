\begin{Exercise}[
name={},
title={}, 
difficulty=0,
origin={\cite{JH}}]
What is the inverse of \( rH \)?
\end{Exercise}
\begin{Answer}
The proof that the inverse is 
          \( r^{-1}H^{-1}=(1/r)\cdot H^{-1} \) 
          (provided, of course, that the matrix is invertible) is easy.
\end{Answer}
