Prove: If $A$ is invertible, then $\left( A^{-1}\right) ^{-1}=A.$ 
\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
Since $A^{-1}A=AA^{-1}=I$ and inverses are unique then it follows that
$\left( A^{-1}\right) ^{-1}=A$.
\end{Answer}
