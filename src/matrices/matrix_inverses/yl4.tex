Given an $n\times n$ matrix $A$ such that $p(A)=0$ where $p(x)=x^3-x^2+1$.  Determine the inverse of $A$ in terms of $A$.

\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
$A^{-1}=A-A^2$
\end{Answer}
