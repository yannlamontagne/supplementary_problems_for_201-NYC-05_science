Consider the matrices
\[A =\begin{mat}
1 & 2 \\
3 & 2 \\
1 & -1
\end{mat},\; B=
\begin{mat}
2 & -5 & 2 \\
-3 & 2 & 1
\end{mat},\; 
C = \begin{mat}
1 & 2 \\
5 & 0
\end{mat},
\]
\[ D=
\begin{mat}
-1 & 1 \\
4 & -3
\end{mat},\; E=
\begin{mat}
1 \\
3
\end{mat}
\]

Find the following if possible. If it is not possible explain why. 
\begin{multicols}{3}
\Question $-3A{^T}$
\Question $3B - A^{T}$
\Question $E^{T}B$
\Question $EE^{T}$
\Question $B^{T}B$
\Question $CA^{T}$
\Question $D^{T}BE$
\EndCurrentQuestion
\end{multicols}
\end{Exercise}
