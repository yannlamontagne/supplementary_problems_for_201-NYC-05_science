\begin{Exercise}[
name={},
title={}, 
difficulty=0,
origin={\cite{JH}}]
   Is this a subspace of \( \polyspace_2 \):\\
    \( \set{a_0+a_1x+a_2x^2\suchthat a_0+2a_1+a_2=4} \)? Justify.
\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
No, it is not closed.
      In particular, it is not closed under scalar multiplication because it
      does not contain the zero polynomial.  

\end{Answer}
