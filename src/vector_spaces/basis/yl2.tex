Given\\
$W=\set{\begin{mat}
a & b\\
c & d
\end{mat}
\suchthat a+2b+3c+4d=0\text{ and } b+c+d=0}$
a subspace of $\matspace_{2\times 2}$. Find a basis $B$ for $W$.


\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
$\set{\begin{mat}
-1 & -1\\
1 & 0
\end{mat},\;
\begin{mat}
-2 & -1\\
0 & 1\\
\end{mat}}$
\end{Answer}
