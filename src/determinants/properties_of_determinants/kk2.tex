\begin{Exercise}[
name={},
title={}, 
difficulty=0,
origin={\cite{KK}}]
Prove or disprove: If $A$ and $B$ are square matrices of the same size then $\detop \left( A+B\right) =\detop \left( A\right) +\detop
\left( B\right)$.
\end{Exercise}
\begin{Answer}
The statement is false.  Consider 
$A=
\begin{mat}
1 & 0 \\
0 & 1
\end{mat}
B=
\begin{mat}
-1 & 0 \\
0 & -1
\end{mat}.$
\end{Answer}
