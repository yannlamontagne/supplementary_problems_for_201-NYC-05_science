\begin{Exercise}[

Solve only for $x_1$ using Cramer's Rule.
\[
\begin{linsys}{3}
x_1 & - & 2x_2 & +  & 3x_3 & = 4\\
   &   & 5x_2 & -  & 6x_3 & = 7\\
   &   &      &    & 8x_3 & = 9
\end{linsys}
\]
\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
$x_1=4$

\end{Answer}
