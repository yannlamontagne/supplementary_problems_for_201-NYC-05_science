\begin{Exercise}[

Graphically, find the point of intersection of the two lines 
$3x+y=3$ and $x+2y=1.$ That is, graph each line
and see where they intersect. 
\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
$\begin{linsys}{3}
3x&+&y&=&3 \\
x&+&2y&=&1
\end{linsys}
$, Solution is: $x=1,i\;y=0$
\end{Answer}
