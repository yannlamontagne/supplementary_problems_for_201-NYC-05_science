Find all vectors of length $2$ that are orthogonal to both $\vec{u}=(-2, 3, 1)$ and $\vec{v}=(1, 2, -3)$.
\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
$\frac{\pm 2}{\sqrt{195}}(-11,\;-5,\;-7)$
