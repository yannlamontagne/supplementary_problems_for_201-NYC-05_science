Given three vectors $\vec{u}=(2,\;1,\;3)$, $\vec{v}=(-1,\;-1,\;1)$, and $\vec{w}=(0,\;1,\;-1)$, find the volume of the parallelepiped defined by $\vec{u}$, $2\vec{v}$, and $3\vec{w}$.
\end{Exercise}

24
