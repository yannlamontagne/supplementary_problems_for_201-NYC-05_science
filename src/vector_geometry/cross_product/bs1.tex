\begin{Exercise}[

Given the points $A(1, 2, -1)$ and $B(1, 1, 2)$.  Find the point $C$ on the $y$-axis such that the area of the triangle $ABC$ is $\frac{\sqrt{10}}{2}$.
\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
$C=\left(0,\;\frac53,\;0\right)$
\end{Answer}
