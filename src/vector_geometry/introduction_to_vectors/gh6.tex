\begin{Exercise}[
name={},
title={}, 
difficulty=0,
origin={\cite{GHC}}]
Under what conditions is $\norm{\vec u}+\norm{\vec v} = \norm{\vec u+\vec v}$?
\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
When $\vec u$ and $\vec v$ have the same direction. (Note: parallel is not enough.)
\end{Answer}
