\begin{Exercise}[

Find a vector $\vec{u}$, of length 3, in the direction of $\vec{PQ}$, where $P(5,1,2)$ and $Q(-2,-2,5)$.
\end{Exercise}
\begin{Answer}
$\vec{u}=\frac{3}{\sqrt{67}}\rowvec{-7,&-3,&3}$
\end{Answer}
