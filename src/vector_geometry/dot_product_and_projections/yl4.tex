Prove: If $\vec{u}, \vec{v}\in \mathbb{R}^n$ such that $||\vec{u}||=\sqrt{2}$ and the angle between $\vec{u}$ and $\vec{v}$ is $\frac{\pi}{4}$ then $||\vec{u}+\vec{v}||^2=||\vec{v}||^2+2||\vec{v}||+2$.
\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
Hint: $||\vec{u}+\vec{v}||^2=(\vec{u}+\vec{v})\cdot(\vec{u}+\vec{v})$
\end{Answer}
