$\mathcal{P}_1: 2x+y-3z=6$,\;

\Question $\mathcal{P}_1$ and $\mathcal{P}_2$ are  $\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_$ to each other.

\end{Exercise}
