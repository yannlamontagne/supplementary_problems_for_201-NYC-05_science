\begin{Exercise}[
name={},

difficulty=0,
origin={\cite{SM}}]
Find the vector equation, and the parametric equations, of the line through $(1,5,-2)$ and $(4,-1,3)$.

\end{Exercise}

\begin{Answer}
$\vec{x} = (1,5,-2)+t(3,-6,5)$, and $\begin{parameqns} x &=& 1+3t \\ y &=& 5-6t \\ z &=& -2+5t \end{parameqns}$, $t\in\Re$.
\end{Answer}
